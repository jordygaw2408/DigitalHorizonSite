Colin Graham Music

A complete digital presence for Northern Ireland's rising country music star

The Challenge

Colin Graham, a rapidly growing Northern Irish country singer with 115,000+ monthly Spotify listeners and 10 million streams, needed a central hub for his online presence. Fans struggled to find tour dates scattered across multiple ticketing platforms (Ticketmaster, Shine, Eventbrite), and Colin lacked a professional platform to showcase his music, story, and upcoming shows.

Our Solution

We created a comprehensive music website that serves as Colin's digital home. The site features a prominent tour dates page consolidating all ticket links in one place, embedded Spotify players and YouTube videos, Colin's compelling personal story, and a professional contact system. The design balances his country music aesthetic with modern functionality, ensuring fans can easily discover his music and buy tickets.

Key Deliverables

  • Complete website design & development
  • Centralized tour dates with multi-platform ticket links
  • Integrated Spotify & YouTube embeds
  • Biography page with personal storytelling
  • Mobile-optimized music player & video gallery
  • GDPR-compliant analytics & cookie consent
