About us

Hi, We're Digital Horizon

A small team with big ambitions, helping local businesses in Northern Ireland build digital presences that actually work. No corporate fluff, just honest web design that drives results.

Meet the Founder

Jordan Gaw

Founder

I started Digital Horizon because I got tired of seeing local businesses being underserved by the digital industry.

After years working with agencies that treated websites like assembly-line products, I knew there was a better approach. One that puts the business owner first, speaks plain English, and focuses ruthlessly on what actually matters - getting you more customers.

I'm not interested in winning design awards or impressing other designers. I care about one thing: does your website make your phone ring? Does it fill your calendar? Does it grow your business?

When you work with Digital Horizon, you're working directly with me. No account managers, no middlemen - just straightforward communication and websites that work as hard as you do.
